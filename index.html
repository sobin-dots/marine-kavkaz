About KAVKAZ Marine

Excellence in Marine Engineering

KAVKAZ MARINE PTE LTD is a Singapore-based marine engineering and ship repair company delivering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions to shipowners, managers, and offshore operators.

With over 12 years of industry experience, we specialize in high-stakes repairs at Singapore anchorage, PSA terminals, and on-voyage locations. Our team is operated by experienced marine engineers with deep shipyard backgrounds.
